CONGRATULATIONS TO THE ALDEAN ARMY NOVEMBER FAN OF THE MONTH

Congrats to the November Aldean Army featured fan contest winner – Nick from Shirleysburg, PA! Read Nick’s fan story and share yours for a chance to win a prize pack and be featured next month.

Congratulations to the Fan of the Month for November – Nick. Learn more about Nick below.

Where are you from? Shrileysburg, PA

What was the first Jason Aldean song you heard? “Hicktown”

How many years have you been a Jason Aldean fan? 16

How many Jason Aldean shows have you been to? 5

What is the farthest you have travelled for a Jason Aldean concert? 1 hour 45 minutes

What’s one thing that sets you apart from other Jason Aldean fans? I was hospitalized in 2004 with a rare form of encephalitis. During my recovery, Hicktown was released and I listened to it non-stop. From then on, I was hooked. I believe your music helped me recover.

Tell us your favorite Jason Aldean related story. This photo is from a meet and greet the first time he performed at the Bryce Jordan Center in State College, PA, but at the concert he didn’t see me coming to the stage and started to walk away. At the last second he saw me out of the corner of his eye and motioned for my mom to take me to the edge of the stage. Then he gave me a guitar pick!

Congratulations go out to Nick, they will be receiving a Jason Aldean prize pack!
